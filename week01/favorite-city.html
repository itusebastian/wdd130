My Favorite City

My favorite city is Ibarra, Ecuador. It is a small mountain town at the foot of the Imbabura volcano. There is so much nature all around, including lakes that people can visit. I love how close it is to some of my favorite places. From Ibarra, we can see other high mountains like Cayambe and Cotacachi, where Lake Cuicocha is located.
